Welcome to My Party!

My Party! is a tracking and discovery application to help revelers and party-goers record their favorite drinks. My Party! allows the user to enter their own favorite beverages, assign a rating, and an occasion. Users can modify the recipes later if they want to improve upon what they already posted.

My Party! is also a social site where beverages are crowd sourced. As beverages are added by users signed up to the site, these beverages become available for viewing by all other members. The more members who join the better selection to choose from.

Where to Start?

It's easy really, just navigate to the Add Beverages pane and you can begin entering your favorite beverages. Can't think of a drink? Well you can search through The Cocktail DB for drink ideas to help you on your way. What? Why not just go there instead?

My Party! is about letting you keep the best beverage recipes on hand and tweak the recipes as you like. Add an occassion and you can start to put together quite the celebration.

Each beverage you enter is shared with the My Party! community. The more members, the more ideas shared! Rememeber though, only you can modify or delete the recipes you put up. So My Party! is dynamic with users sharing and updating their favorite beverages all the time!
